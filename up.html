Varanasi

Varanasi, one of the oldest living cities in the world, is a spiritual hub for Hindus. The ghats along the Ganges River, the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, and the evening Ganga Aarti make it a must-visit for those seeking spiritual experiences.

Lucknow

Lucknow, the city of Nawabs, is known for its rich cultural heritage, Mughlai cuisine, and historic monuments like the Bara Imambara and Chota Imambara. The city also boasts an exquisite blend of old-world charm and modernity.
