Marina Beach

Located in Chennai, Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world. It's famous for its stunning sunrise views, bustling promenade, and local street food. A perfect place to enjoy a peaceful evening by the sea.

Meenakshi Amman Temple

The Meenakshi Amman Temple, located in Madurai, is a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture. Dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Shiva, this iconic temple is renowned for its towering gopurams and intricate carvings.

Ooty - The Queen of Hill Stations

Ooty, or Udhagamandalam, is a picturesque hill station located in the Nilgiris. Known for its sprawling tea plantations, cool climate, and scenic beauty, Ooty is a favorite destination for honeymooners and nature lovers.

Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari, located at the southernmost tip of India, is known for its stunning sunrises and sunsets. The meeting point of the Indian Ocean, Arabian Sea, and Bay of Bengal, it is also home to the iconic Vivekananda Rock Memorial.
