Mysore Palace

The Mysore Palace, located in the heart of Mysuru, is a magnificent example of Indo-Saracenic architecture. Famous for its intricate carvings and vibrant illumination during Dussehra, the palace attracts millions of visitors every year.

Coorg - The Scotland of India

Coorg, or Kodagu, is a beautiful hill station known for its coffee plantations, misty landscapes, and vibrant culture. It's an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an ancient city filled with stunning ruins and architectural marvels. It was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is renowned for its temples, sculptures, and rock formations.

Bandipur National Park

Bandipur National Park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts. Home to tigers, elephants, and a variety of other species, this park offers breathtaking safaris and a chance to experience nature in its purest form.
