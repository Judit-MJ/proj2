Srinagar

Srinagar, the summer capital of Jammu & Kashmir, is famous for its stunning Dal Lake, houseboats, and Mughal gardens. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains, it’s a paradise for nature lovers.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg, often called the "Meadow of Flowers," is a premier skiing destination in India. It offers breathtaking views of snow-clad peaks and lush meadows during summer.

Pahalgam

Pahalgam, located on the banks of the Lidder River, is known for its pristine landscapes, pine forests, and the starting point for the Amarnath Yatra. It’s a haven for adventure and trekking enthusiasts.

Leh-Ladakh

Leh-Ladakh is a land of stunning landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and serene lakes like Pangong Tso. It’s a perfect destination for road trips, adventure sports, and cultural exploration.
