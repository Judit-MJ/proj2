Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is known for its vibrant culture, palaces, and forts. The Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal are some of its iconic landmarks, offering a glimpse into the royal past of India.

Udaipur

Udaipur, often called the "City of Lakes," is famous for its beautiful lakes, palaces, and temples. The City Palace and Lake Pichola offer serene views, making it a romantic and picturesque destination.

Jodhpur

Jodhpur, known as the "Blue City," is renowned for its imposing Mehrangarh Fort, vibrant markets, and traditional havelis. The fort offers panoramic views of the city, with its blue-painted houses creating a stunning visual contrast.
